03 — Undergraduate research project

Undergraduate research project.

GENE — SLC4A11

Histological Analysis and SLC4A11 Gene Expression After Injection of Umbilical Cord Mesenchymal Stem Cells in a Rabbit (Oryctolagus cuniculus, Linnaeus 1758) Model of Corneal Endothelial Lesions.

Methods
H&E Histology, qPCR, Cell Culture
Model
Oryctolagus cuniculus (rabbit)
Status
In progress — 2026

This study evaluates whether umbilical-cord-derived mesenchymal stem cells (UC-MSC) can support corneal endothelial repair, using a rabbit model of corneal endothelial lesions. It combines histological evaluation of corneal tissue with expression analysis of SLC4A11 — a gene tied to endothelial pump function — to assess regeneration after UC-MSC injection.

UC-MSC injection Histology Gene expression / qPCR Corneal endothelium Animal model Regenerative medicine
